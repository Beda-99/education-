GRAMMAR WRITING VOCABULARY READING

Grammar & Structure (Lessons 01-08)

01 Passive Voice

Focus on the object.

Subject + Be + V3

Example: The exam was marked by the teacher.

02 Reported Speech

Repeating what was said.

"I am happy" → He said he was happy.

03 Conditional Type 1

Real possibilities.

If + Present, Will + Verb

If you study, you will pass.

04 Relative Clauses

Who (people), Which (things).

The man who built this site is Mr. Vue.

05 Question Tags

Confirmation.

It's a sunny day, isn't it?

06 Adverbs

Describe verbs.

He ran quickly to catch the bus.

07 Conjunctions

Linking words.

Although, because, despite, and, but.

08 Prepositions

Location/Time.

At, on, in, behind, under, across.

Vocabulary Mastery (Lessons 09-14)

09 Synonyms

Same meanings.

Start/Begin, Finish/End, Happy/Glad.

10 Antonyms

Opposites.

Success/Failure, Brave/Cowardly.

11 Animal Young

Cat: Kitten | Dog: Puppy | Hen: Chick.

12 Similes

As busy as a bee | As white as snow.

13 Proverbs

"Action speaks louder than words."

14 Collective Nouns

A flock of birds | A pride of lions.

Functional Writing (Lessons 15-18)

15 Formal Letter

Use "Yours faithfully" and address the title.

16 Informal Letter

Use "Dear Friend" and "Your loving son".

17 Report Writing

State facts clearly and use past tense.

18 Directions

Turn right, go past, opposite the market.
