Reading & Books

Improve your reading skills with our guide and recommended Kinyarwanda books for learners at all levels.

Reading Practice Text

Muraho! Nitwa John. Ndi umunyarwanda. Mfite imyaka mirongo ibiri. Ndi umunyeshuri. Nkunda kusoma ibitabo no gusoma amasomo. Uyu munsi, hari izuba rikomeye. Ngiye mu ishuri.

Translation: Hello! My name is John. I am Rwandan. I am twenty years old. I am a student. I like reading books and studying. Today, the sun is shining brightly. I am going to school.
