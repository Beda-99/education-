General FAQ

Are these resources updated for the CBC curriculum? +

Yes. All textbooks and teaching materials are strictly aligned with the Rwanda Basic Education Board (REB) Competence-Based Curriculum (CBC).

Can I use Becon EducationðŸ–± on my mobile phone? +

Absolutely. Our platform is optimized for mobile viewing. You can read books and download lab manuals directly to your smartphone.

Is there a cost for TVET/TSS manuals? +

No. Our mission is free accessibility. Technical manuals for Software Development, Masonry, Electricity, and others are provided at zero cost.

How do I contribute my own study notes? +

We love community input! Visit the Contact page and select "Resource Contribution" to share your materials with our administrative team for review.

Why won't my PDF download? +

Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If the issue persists, clear your browser cache or try using the 'Save Link As' option by right-clicking the download button.
